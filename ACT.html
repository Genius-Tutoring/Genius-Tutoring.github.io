ACT English Preparation

The ACT English section evaluates grammar, punctuation, sentence structure, and rhetorical skills. It's crucial for understanding and editing written English.

  • Length: 45-minute English Test
  • Categories:
    • Usage/Mechanics
    • Rhetorical Skills

Our strategy focuses on grammar rules, effective writing strategies, and understanding the nuances of English usage.

ACT Math Preparation

The ACT Math section covers various mathematical topics. It assesses numerical, algebraic, geometric, and statistical problem-solving abilities.

  • Length: 60-minute Math Test
  • Categories:
    • Pre-Algebra/Elementary Algebra
    • Intermediate Algebra/Coordinate Geometry
    • Plane Geometry/Trigonometry

Our approach includes a focus on essential math concepts, problem-solving skills, and application of mathematical reasoning.

ACT Science Preparation

The ACT Science section tests interpretation, analysis, evaluation, reasoning, and problem-solving skills in the natural sciences.

  • Length: 35-minute Science Test
  • Categories:
    • Data Representation
    • Research Summaries
    • Conflicting Viewpoints

Our methodology involves building skills in scientific analysis and interpretation, as well as understanding complex graphs and data.

ACT Writing Preparation

The optional ACT Writing section measures writing skills taught in high school English classes and entry-level college composition courses.

  • Length: 40-minute Essay
  • Focus: Writing a well-organized, coherent, and developed argumentative essay

Our strategy emphasizes structured essay writing, critical thinking, and developing arguments with supporting evidence.

Our Method of Attack

Let's break down the SAT prep process into manageable steps.

Diagnostic Exam

We begin with a comprehensive diagnostic exam to assess the student's current skill level and understand their specific needs.

Performance Tracking:

A pivotal part of our method involves tracking what the student gets right or wrong. This enables us to identify their weaknesses and the topics they struggle with, thereby customizing our teaching approach accordingly.

Interim Exams:

Regular interim exams are conducted to measure student progress. These exams simulate real test conditions and help in adjusting strategies and focus areas as needed.

Time-Based Preparation:

We emphasize time management skills, helping students to effectively navigate through the test within the given timeframe. This includes practicing under timed conditions and developing strategies to handle the pressure of the exam.
